About Us

Prepaid taps are a type of water tap that requires users to pay for water in advance for people who cannot afford to pay for a monthly water bill.
They are more efficient, as they help to reduce water waste,they are more reliable, as they are not affected by power outages or water shortages.

MISSION

To enable customers pay only for what they can consume.

VISION

To provide people with more reliable, clean, affordable water.
